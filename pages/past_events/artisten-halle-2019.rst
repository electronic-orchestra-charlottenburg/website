Info
####

On 8th March 2019 the EOC played at the Artisten-Halle, Berlin on their
(surprisingly bassy) eight-channel speaker system. The evening was shared with
solo performances by **AA..LL**, **1)3\\/1532** and **TRUMMERSCHLUNK**.
